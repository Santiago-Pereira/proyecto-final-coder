Workout with Me!

You can have a virtual 1-on-1 training session with me. Delivered to you at home (or anywhere) over the internet, using video conferencing tools like Zoom™, Whatsapp™ or Skype™.

I'm going to show you how to do every exercise in your personal rutine, motivate you, giving you tips to improve everyday, and overall, stay there for anything you may need!.

Getting Started

To get started I recommend an Online Trial Session. In this session you will meet me as your online trainer before making an ongoing commitment.

I will review your medical screening form, complete a fitness assessment, help you set realistic goals and complete a mini workout to give you an idea of what working with me is like.

The fitness test helps to establish your current level of fitness of which you will receive a report at the end of the session.
