About Me

My interest in sports started when I was very young and diagnosed with ADHD (attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder). I just couldn’t stand still anywhere, to which a psychiatrist recommended my parents I started performing energy demanding physical activity as treatment to avoid medication.

This way a long road of trying out different sports, because I liked to have different experiences, began.

I tried swimming, running, karate, boxing and so on. Eventually, I had to choose a career path, so I decided to study Exercise Science at Griffith University preluded by a Health Science Diploma at Griffith College, where I realized my desire to help people achieve their desired physiques and improve their health all while minimizing their risk of injury during training.

This way I have created a platform with specific programs depending on people’s goals.
